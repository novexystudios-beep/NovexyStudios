Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workflow and policies.

What types of videos do you edit?
We specialize in:
  • All YouTube content
  • Instagram Reels
  • Tiktok videos
Do you provide music and stock footage?
Yes. We use high-quality royalty-free music and stock footage to enhance your video without copyright issues.
Does this include color grading and sound?
Yes. Every package includes professional color correction and audio mixing to ensure your video looks and sounds crisp.
How do I send you my footage?
We use Google Drive. You’ll receive a secure private link to upload all your raw files easily.
How long does the editing take?
Standard delivery: 24–48 hours for short-form. Long-form takes 2-6 days depending on length. Rush delivery (24h) is available for an additional fee.
Can I make changes to the video?
Yes. Revisions may extend the final delivery time by 24-48 hours, depending on the scope and complexity of the requested changes.
How does your pricing work?
  • Per-Project Rates (pay as you go)
  • Monthly Retainers (discounted for consistent creators)
Do you require a deposit?
Yes. We require a 50% deposit to secure your slot in our schedule. The remaining 50% after you approve the final video.
Are there any hidden fees?
Never. The quoted price is final unless you request work outside the original scope.
Do I get the editable project files?
We deliver the final MP4/MOV. Editable project files (Premiere/DaVinci) can be provided.
Is my footage private?
Absolutely. Your files stay 100% confidential and never shared with anyone.
What if I don't like the result?
We use your included revision rounds to adjust the edit until it matches your vision
Do you use AI or real editors?
Your videos are crafted by professional human editors. AI is used only for minor speed-up tasks — never for creativity or storytelling.
What happens if I lose my video later?
We keep a secure backup of your final video for 1 month after delivery. If you lose your copy, just email us and we will resend it for free.
